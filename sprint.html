Design sprint Ashtanga Yoga

  • Category: Design Sprint
  • Client: Code Academy
  • Project date: 01 May, 2021
  • Personal Role: Decider

The team task was to create the Ashtanga yoga app which without human interference would show how to do the exercises and evaluate whether they are doing well.

01 Map

We created a mind map, generated ideas, and decided our target audience.

02 Sketch

in this section, we searched for inspirations and did individual "crazy 8".

03 Decide

In this section, we decided which ideas are best for our app. We compiled a list of elements based on the voted ideas and prepared for the development of a prototype.

04 Prototype

In the prototype stage, we all created this app and criticize the imperfections, for best results.

05 Test

Test stage was when one of us, created tasks for the app and tested with the potential users.
